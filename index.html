Power of scent

What comes to your mind when referred to coffee, rose or jasmine? Are you not feeling the smell? Chances are very high that you remember persons based on the scent they use.

The delicious scent of baked bread or the aroma that emanates from the backstreet café makes you buy those, even if you intend not to, right? That’s the power of scents!

Research shows that the sense of smell is closely associated with memory, probably more so than any of our other senses. More than a trigger of memories, they influence your emotions too. You may not want to stay long when the ambient smell is not welcoming.

ZCENTPRO

ZcentPro aroma diffusers are elegantly designed diffusers that takes 150 ml to 1000 ml refill bottle and covers areas from 300 CBM to 1200 CBM. The machine has two color variations, black or white. Easy to refill and very efficient.

CARZCENT

Carzcent diffuser is for your car or home/office desktop. Powered by USB power output, this diffuser can scent up to 100 sq.ft. The multi-level control helps you to control the intensity of scent.

ZCENTMax100

ZCENTMax100 is the best ones when it comes to spaces that are around 120 CBM. This battery operated diffuser can take 100ml refill aroma oil.

SCENTED CANDLES

Zcentz scented candles helps you to create a celestial ambiance. Unique scents and beautiful presentation will enhance the ambience of your interior. Scented candles are the perfect gift for all occasions. Zcentz can brand and customize the candles when ordered in bulk.

ZCENTAIR

HVAC Scent systems are for larger spaces. ZcentAir is one of the perfect scent system for HVAC. ZCENTZ has a very unique system of connecting the diffuser to the duct system.
